Harry's Ordinary Life: 11-year-old Harry lives miserably with Aunt Petunia, Uncle Vernon, and bullying cousin Dudley Dursley in a cupboard under the stairs.

Magical Discovery: Giant Hagrid reveals Harry is a wizard whose parents were killed by evil Lord Voldemort; Harry receives his Hogwarts letter.

Diagon Alley Adventure: Harry visits magical shopping street, gets wand (phoenix feather matches Voldemort's), owl Hedwig, and discovers he's famous as "The Boy Who Lived."

Hogwarts Arrival: Sorting Hat places Harry in Gryffindor house with friendly Ron Weasley; bossy Hermione Granger joins them.

Flying Lessons & Quidditch: Harry excels as youngest Seeker ever; his Nimbus 2000 broom is sabotaged during first match (Snape suspected).

Troll Incident: Hermione locked with mountain troll in girls' bathroom; Harry/Ron save her, cementing friendship trio.
